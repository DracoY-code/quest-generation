<|begin_quest|>

<|end_objective|>
<|begin_tasks|>
Find a super repair kit, which can be located in New Reno, Vault City, or NCR.
Return the super repair kit to Skeeter in Gecko.
<|end_tasks|>
<|begin_quest_giver|>
Skeeter: A ghoul mechanic in Gecko who can improve your Highwayman.
<|end_quest_giver|>
<|begin_task_locations|>
New Reno: A lawless city controlled by mob families, known for its underground dealings.
Vault City: A highly advanced settlement with strict laws and valuable technology.
NCR: A growing republic aiming to bring law and order to the wasteland.
Gecko: A ghoul-run town with a nuclear power plant, where Skeeter resides.
<|end_task_locations|>
<|begin_rewards|>
Car Upgrade: Skeeter will enhance the Highwayman, improving its efficiency. (amount: NONE)
<|end_rewards|>
<|end_quest|>
