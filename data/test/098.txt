<|begin_quest|>
<|begin_objective|>
Repair the air purifier in Redding’s mine to ensure safe working conditions for the miners.
<|end_objective|>
<|begin_tasks|>
Speak to Marge LeBarge about the malfunctioning air purifier.
Obtain the necessary parts to fix the purifier.
Locate the air purifier within the mine.
Repair the purifier to restore clean airflow.
<|end_tasks|>
<|begin_quest_giver|>
Marge LeBarge: The manager of Redding’s mine, concerned about the safety of the workers.
<|end_quest_giver|>
<|begin_task_locations|>
Redding: A mining town where workers rely on the air purifier for safe working conditions.
Redding Mine: The site of the malfunctioning air purifier, filled with miners and possible threats.
<|end_task_locations|>
<|begin_rewards|>
Experience Points: Earned for successfully repairing the air purifier. (amount: 1500)
Caps: A monetary reward from Marge LeBarge for fixing the purifier. (amount: 500)
<|end_rewards|>
<|end_quest|>
<|begin_description|>
The air purifier in Redding’s mine has broken down, posing a serious health risk to the miners. Marge LeBarge has asked you to find the necessary parts and repair it to ensure their safety.
<|end_description|>
