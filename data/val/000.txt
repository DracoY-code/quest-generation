<|begin_quest|>
<|begin_objective|>
Find a piece of flint so Mynoc can sharpen your spear.
<|end_objective|>
<|begin_tasks|>
Locate someone in Arroyo who has flint.
Acquire the flint through trade or persuasion.
Bring the flint to Mynoc for sharpening.
<|end_tasks|>
<|begin_quest_giver|>
Mynoc: A skilled tribal craftsman who can improve weapons.
<|end_quest_giver|>
<|begin_task_locations|>
Arroyo Village: A small tribal village where Mynoc resides.
<|end_task_locations|>
<|begin_rewards|>
Sharpened Spear: An improved version of the standard spear with better damage. (amount: 1)
<|end_rewards|>
<|end_quest|>
Mynoc, a craftsman in Arroyo, offers to sharpen your spear if you can bring him a piece of flint. The flint can be obtained from another villager through trade or conversation.
