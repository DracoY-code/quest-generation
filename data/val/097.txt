<|begin_quest|>
<|begin_objective|>
Persuade the Brotherhood of Steel Elders to provide aid for an upcoming conflict.
<|end_objective|>
<|begin_tasks|>
Speak with the Elders to present your case.
Provide evidence or arguments to convince them.
Await their decision on whether to send assistance.
<|end_tasks|>
<|begin_quest_giver|>
Concerned Brotherhood Member: A Brotherhood member who believes help should be sent.
<|end_quest_giver|>
<|begin_task_locations|>
Brotherhood of Steel Bunker: The main headquarters where the Elders hold council meetings.
<|end_task_locations|>
<|begin_rewards|>
Brotherhood Support: If successful, the Brotherhood may send aid in the form of supplies or soldiers. (amount: NONE)
Experience Points: Earned upon successfully persuading the Elders. (amount: 1000)
<|end_rewards|>
<|end_quest|>
A dire situation requires the Brotherhood of Steel's assistance. You must convince the Elders to intervene by presenting a strong case for their involvement. Your success depends on your persuasive skills and available evidence.
