<|begin_quest|>
<|begin_objective|>
Convince the rebel faction in Vault 13 to remain within the vault.
<|end_objective|>
<|begin_tasks|>
Speak to upset Vault 13 citizens to learn about the rebel meetings.
<|end_tasks|>
<|begin_quest_giver|>
Upset Vault Dweller: A concerned resident expressing dissatisfaction with the current situation.
<|end_quest_giver|>
<|begin_task_locations|>
Vault 13 Living Quarters: Residential area where Vault 13 inhabitants reside.
<|end_task_locations|>
<|begin_rewards|>
Experience Points: Awarded for successfully convincing the rebels to stay. (amount: 750)
<|end_rewards|>
<|end_quest|>
<|begin_description|>
Upon returning to Vault 13 during the search for the water chip, the Vault Dweller discovers unrest among the citizens. Some residents, led by Theresa, are contemplating leaving the safety of the vault to face the unknowns of the wasteland. By engaging with the upset dwellers and attending their meeting at 17:00 in Theresa's quarters, the Vault Dweller has an opportunity to persuade them to remain, ensuring the stability and unity of the vault community.
<|end_description|>
