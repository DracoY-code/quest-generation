<|begin_quest|>
<|begin_objective|>
Rescue the slaves held by the Khans.
<|end_objective|>
<|begin_tasks|>
Locate the slaves in the Khans' camp.
Eliminate or negotiate with the Khans guarding them.
Unlock or break open their restraints to free them.
<|end_tasks|>
<|begin_quest_giver|>
None (Optional Quest): The player can choose to free the slaves without a direct quest giver.
<|end_quest_giver|>
<|begin_task_locations|>
Khans' Camp: A raider stronghold where the Khans keep their captives.
<|end_task_locations|>
<|begin_rewards|>
Karma Increase: The player's reputation improves for freeing the slaves. (amount: 1)
<|end_rewards|>
<|end_quest|>
The player can attempt to free the slaves held by the Khans through combat or stealth. This action improves the player's moral standing but may provoke hostility from the Khans.
