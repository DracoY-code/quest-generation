<|begin_quest|>
<|begin_objective|>
Find and bring back the Garden of Eden Creation Kit (GECK) to save Arroyo.
<|end_objective|>
<|begin_tasks|>
Travel to Vault City to inquire about the GECK.
Gain access to Vault 8.
Search the Vault for the GECK.
Find an alternative source if the GECK is unavailable.
<|end_tasks|>
<|begin_quest_giver|>
Arroyo Elder: The leader of Arroyo who tasks you with finding the GECK to restore the village.
<|end_quest_giver|>
<|begin_task_locations|>
Vault City: A technologically advanced settlement that may have information on the GECK.
<|end_task_locations|>
<|begin_rewards|>
Experience Points: Earned for finding the GECK. (amount: 4000)
<|end_rewards|>
<|end_quest|>
<|begin_description|>
Arroyo is in desperate need of the GECK, a device that can revitalize barren lands. You must search Vault City and beyond to locate it and ensure the survival of your home.
<|end_description|>
