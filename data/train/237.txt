<|begin_objective|>
Investigate the disappearances in Broken Hills and find out what happened to the missing people.
<|end_objective|>
<|begin_tasks|>
Talk to Marcus in Broken Hills to learn about the missing people.
Search for clues around Broken Hills and interview residents.
Investigate the mine to uncover any evidence.
Confront those responsible and decide their fate.
<|end_tasks|>
<|begin_quest_giver|>
Marcus: A super mutant sheriff in Broken Hills who wants to maintain peace in the town.
<|end_quest_giver|>
<|begin_task_locations|>
Broken Hills: A town where super mutants, ghouls, and humans live together. The disappearances are causing tension among the residents.
Broken Hills Mine: A dangerous area suspected to be linked to the missing people case.
<|end_task_locations|>
<|begin_rewards|>
Experience Points: Earned for solving the case and bringing justice. (amount: 2500)
Caps: A monetary reward from Marcus for uncovering the truth. (amount: 500)
<|end_rewards|>
