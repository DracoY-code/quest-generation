<|begin_quest|>
<|begin_objective|>
Retrieve a map from the NCR Rangers and deliver it to Vortis, a slaver in NCR.
<|end_objective|>
<|begin_tasks|>
Locate the NCR Rangers and find a way to acquire the map.
Steal, persuade, or fight to obtain the map.
Return the map to Vortis in NCR for your reward.
<|end_tasks|>
<|begin_quest_giver|>
Vortis: A known slaver in NCR who wants to acquire the map for his own purposes.
<|end_quest_giver|>
<|begin_task_locations|>
NCR Ranger Safehouse: A hidden location where the NCR Rangers store sensitive information, including the map.
Vortis' Hideout: A slaver base in NCR where Vortis operates.
<|end_task_locations|>
<|begin_rewards|>
Caps: Payment from Vortis upon delivering the map. (amount: 500)
Slaver Reputation: Increased standing with slavers for helping Vortis. (amount: NONE)
<|end_rewards|>
<|end_quest|>
Vortis, a slaver in NCR, wants a map currently held by the NCR Rangers. He tasks you with retrieving it through any means necessary, offering a financial reward upon completion. Be prepared for potential conflict with the Rangers if discovered.
