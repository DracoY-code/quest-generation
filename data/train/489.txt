<|begin_quest|>
<|begin_objective|>
Prove your worth to the Brotherhood of Steel to join as an Initiate.
<|end_objective|>
<|begin_tasks|>
Speak with Cabbot at the entrance of the Brotherhood bunker.
Accept the challenge to retrieve a holodisk from the Glow.
Survive the journey to the Glow and retrieve the required holodisk.
<|end_tasks|>
<|begin_quest_giver|>
Cabbot: A Brotherhood of Steel guard stationed at the bunker entrance.
<|end_quest_giver|>
<|begin_task_locations|>
Brotherhood of Steel Bunker: The heavily fortified headquarters of the Brotherhood of Steel.
The Glow (West Tek Research Facility): An irradiated pre-war research complex filled with dangerous obstacles.
<|end_task_locations|>
<|begin_rewards|>
Brotherhood of Steel Membership: Grants access to the Brotherhood facilities and resources. (amount: NONE)
Experience Points: Earned upon successful completion. (amount: 1500)
<|end_rewards|>
<|end_quest|>
<|begin_description|>
The Brotherhood of Steel requires proof of dedication before accepting new members. To join, you must retrieve a holodisk from the Glow, a dangerous and heavily irradiated pre-war research facility.
<|end_description|>
