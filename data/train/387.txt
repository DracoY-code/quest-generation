<|begin_quest|>
<|begin_objective|>
Save Sinthia from a violent thug in the Skum Pitt.
<|end_objective|>
<|begin_tasks|>
Enter the Skum Pitt bar.
Confront the thug threatening Sinthia.
Decide whether to de-escalate, fight, or negotiate.
<|end_tasks|>
<|begin_quest_giver|>
None (Triggered by Event): The quest starts when the player witnesses Sinthia in distress.
<|end_quest_giver|>
<|begin_task_locations|>
Skum Pitt: A bar in Junktown where Sinthia is being threatened.
<|end_task_locations|>
<|begin_rewards|>
Karma Increase: Rescuing Sinthia improves the player's reputation. (amount: 1)
<|end_rewards|>
<|end_quest|>
While visiting the Skum Pitt, the player encounters Sinthia being harassed by a thug. The situation can be resolved through persuasion, intimidation, or combat. Successfully helping Sinthia improves the player's standing in Junktown.
