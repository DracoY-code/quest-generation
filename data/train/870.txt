<|begin_quest|>
<|begin_objective|>
Rescue a Brotherhood of Steel initiate captured by enemies in The Hub.
<|end_objective|>
<|begin_tasks|>
Gather intelligence on the initiate's location.
Infiltrate the enemy hideout in The Hub.
Defeat or negotiate with the captors.
Escort the initiate safely out of the hideout.
<|end_tasks|>
<|begin_quest_giver|>
Brotherhood of Steel Member: A representative of the Brotherhood seeking help in rescuing their captured initiate.
<|end_quest_giver|>
<|begin_task_locations|>
The Hub: A major trading city where the Brotherhood initiate is being held captive.
<|end_task_locations|>
<|begin_rewards|>
Caps: A monetary reward for rescuing the initiate. (amount: 500)
Experience Points: Earned for successfully completing the rescue operation. (amount: 1000)
Brotherhood Reputation: Increases standing with the Brotherhood of Steel, unlocking possible future benefits. (amount: 1)
<|end_rewards|>
<|end_quest|>
A Brotherhood of Steel initiate has been captured in The Hub. The player must locate the captive, engage in combat or negotiations with the captors, and ensure the initiate returns safely to the Brotherhood.
