<|begin_quest|>
<|begin_objective|>
Gain access to a restricted armory to acquire powerful weapons and equipment.
<|end_objective|>
<|begin_tasks|>
Find a way to bypass or unlock the armory doors.
<|end_tasks|>
<|begin_quest_giver|>
Self-initiated or Faction Leader: The need for better equipment drives the Vault Dweller to seek access, or a faction leader assigns the task.
<|end_quest_giver|>
<|begin_task_locations|>
Military Base: A heavily fortified location containing high-grade weapons and armor.
Vault Armory: A secure storage area within a vault, filled with pre-war supplies.
<|end_task_locations|>
<|begin_rewards|>
Advanced Weaponry: Powerful weapons such as energy rifles, explosives, or heavy firearms. (amount: 1)
High-Quality Armor: Stronger defensive gear to improve survivability. (amount: 1)
Additional Supplies: Ammunition, medical supplies, and explosives. (amount: 1)
<|end_rewards|>
<|end_quest|>
<|begin_description|>
The Vault Dweller must find a way to enter a secured armory, either through skill, deception, or force. The armory holds valuable supplies that can significantly impact survival and combat effectiveness.
<|end_description|>
