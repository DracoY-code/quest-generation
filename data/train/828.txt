<|begin_quest|>
<|begin_objective|>
Protect a caravan from threats during its journey.
<|end_objective|>
<|begin_tasks|>
Speak with the caravan master to accept the job.
Prepare for potential attacks.
Escort the caravan to its destination.
<|end_tasks|>
<|begin_quest_giver|>
Caravan Master: A merchant responsible for organizing trade routes and hiring guards.
<|end_quest_giver|>
<|begin_task_locations|>
The Hub: The central trading hub where caravan jobs are offered.
Caravan Route: A dangerous road where the caravan might face raider or mutant attacks.
<|end_task_locations|>
<|begin_rewards|>
Caps: Payment for successfully protecting the caravan. (amount: 600)
Experience Points: Earned for completing the escort mission. (amount: 1000)
<|end_rewards|>
<|end_quest|>
<|begin_description|>
The player is hired to protect a caravan traveling between major settlements. The journey is fraught with dangers, including raiders and mutants, requiring the player to stay alert and defend the caravan against potential threats.
<|end_description|>
