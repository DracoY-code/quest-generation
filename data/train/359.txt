<|begin_objective|>
Help Manson and Franc escape from the NCR prison in Fallout 2.
<|end_objective|>
<|begin_tasks|>
Talk to Manson in the NCR prison to learn about the escape plan.
Disable or distract the guards to create an opportunity for escape.
Unlock the prison cells using lockpicking or a key.
Ensure Manson and Franc safely exit the prison without getting caught.
<|end_tasks|>
<|begin_quest_giver|>
Manson: A prisoner who wants to escape, but needs assistance.
<|end_quest_giver|>
<|begin_task_locations|>
NCR Prison: A secure facility in the New California Republic where Manson and Franc are being held.
<|end_task_locations|>
<|begin_rewards|>
Experience Points: Earned for successfully freeing Manson and Franc. (amount: 1000)
Caps: A monetary reward from Manson and Franc for helping them escape. (amount: 500)
<|end_rewards|>
<|end_quest|>
