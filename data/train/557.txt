<|begin_quest|>
<|begin_objective|>
Investigate and put an end to the brahmin raids plaguing the local ranchers.
<|end_objective|>
<|begin_tasks|>
Speak with the ranchers to gather information about the raids.
Track down the source of the attacks.
Deal with the raiders or creatures responsible for the raids.
Report back to the ranchers after resolving the problem.
<|end_tasks|>
<|begin_quest_giver|>
Concerned Rancher: A local brahmin herder worried about the continuous attacks.
<|end_quest_giver|>
<|begin_task_locations|>
Rancher Settlement: A small community relying on brahmin herding for survival.
Raiders' Hideout: A potential location where raiders may be organizing the attacks.
<|end_task_locations|>
<|begin_rewards|>
Caps: Payment for stopping the raids. (amount: 1000)
Rancher Reputation: Increased standing with the local ranchers. (amount: NONE)
Possibly Loot: If raiders are involved, their supplies may be up for grabs. (amount: NONE)
<|end_rewards|>
<|end_quest|>
<|begin_description|>
The local ranchers are struggling with repeated attacks on their brahmin herds. Your task is to investigate, find out who or what is responsible, and put an end to the raids.
<|end_description|>
