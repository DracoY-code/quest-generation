<|begin_quest|>
<|begin_objective|>
Rescue the enslaved individuals from the slaver pen and return them to the NCR Rangers.
<|end_objective|>
<|begin_tasks|>
Locate the slave pen controlled by slavers.
Find a way to unlock the pen (stealth, persuasion, or combat).
Escort the freed slaves to safety or inform the NCR Rangers of their escape.
<|end_tasks|>
<|begin_quest_giver|>
NCR Rangers: A faction dedicated to justice and anti-slavery operations.
<|end_quest_giver|>
<|begin_task_locations|>
Slave Pen: A heavily guarded area where captured individuals are kept by slavers.
NCR Rangers Outpost: The headquarters where the NCR Rangers coordinate anti-slavery efforts.
<|end_task_locations|>
<|begin_rewards|>
Caps: A payment for successfully freeing the slaves. (amount: 1000)
NCR Ranger Reputation: Increased standing with the NCR Rangers for aiding their cause. (amount: NONE)
Karma Boost: A positive increase in karma for taking a just action. (amount: NONE)
<|end_rewards|>
<|end_quest|>
<|begin_description|>
The NCR Rangers have tasked you with infiltrating a slaver compound and freeing the captives. You can approach the task through stealth, persuasion, or combat, but be mindful of the risks involved. Completing this quest strengthens your ties with the NCR and boosts your karma.
<|end_description|>
