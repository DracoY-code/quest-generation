<|begin_quest|>
<|begin_objective|>
Explore and map the northern wasteland for the Brotherhood of Steel.
<|end_objective|>
<|begin_tasks|>
Speak with the Brotherhood representative to receive scouting orders.
Travel north into the wasteland and record notable locations.
Return to the Brotherhood and report findings.
<|end_tasks|>
<|begin_quest_giver|>
Brotherhood Scout Leader: A Brotherhood officer responsible for gathering intelligence on the wasteland.
<|end_quest_giver|>
<|begin_task_locations|>
Brotherhood of Steel Bunker: The headquarters of the Brotherhood of Steel, where scouting orders are given.
Northern Wasteland: An uncharted region filled with potential threats and points of interest.
<|end_task_locations|>
<|begin_rewards|>
Brotherhood Reputation Boost: Increases standing with the Brotherhood of Steel. (amount: NONE)
Experience Points: Earned upon successful completion. (amount: 750)
<|end_rewards|>
<|end_quest|>
<|begin_description|>
The Brotherhood of Steel needs updated intelligence on the northern wasteland. Your mission is to travel into the unknown, observe points of interest, and return with a report. The journey may be dangerous, so preparation is key.
<|end_description|>
