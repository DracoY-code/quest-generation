<|begin_quest|>
<|begin_objective|>
Prevent the assassination of Killian Darkwater, the mayor and shopkeeper of Junktown.
<|end_objective|>
<|begin_tasks|>
Speak with Killian in his store.
Witness the assassination attempt by a hired gun.
Stop the assassin before he kills Killian.
<|end_tasks|>
<|begin_quest_giver|>
Killian Darkwater: The mayor and general store owner of Junktown who is targeted for assassination.
<|end_quest_giver|>
<|begin_task_locations|>
Darkwater General Store: Killian's shop in Junktown, where the assassination attempt occurs.
<|end_task_locations|>
<|begin_rewards|>
Caps: A monetary reward for stopping the assassin. (amount: 400)
Discount at Darkwater General Store: Killian offers better prices on his wares after being saved. (amount: 1)
<|end_rewards|>
<|end_quest|>
<|begin_description|>
An assassin, hired by Gizmo, attempts to kill Killian in his store. The player must intervene and stop the attack. Successfully saving Killian allows the player to gain his trust and potentially work with him against Gizmo.
<|end_description|>
