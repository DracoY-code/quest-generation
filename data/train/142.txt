<|begin_quest|>
<|begin_objective|>
Help Phil retrieve his lost dog, Roxie.
<|end_objective|>
<|begin_tasks|>
Talk to Phil to learn about Roxie’s disappearance.
Search the surrounding area for Roxie.
Lead Roxie back to Phil safely.
<|end_tasks|>
<|begin_quest_giver|>
Phil: A worried dog owner who needs help finding his missing pet.
<|end_quest_giver|>
<|begin_task_locations|>

Nearby Junkyard: A potential location where Roxie might be found.
<|end_task_locations|>
<|begin_rewards|>
Caps: Phil’s gratitude in the form of a small sum of money. (amount: 100)
Karma Increase: Helping Phil improves the player's reputation. (amount: 1)
<|end_rewards|>
<|end_quest|>
Phil in Junktown has lost his beloved dog, Roxie. The player must search for the missing canine, either by following clues or asking around. Successfully bringing Roxie back to Phil rewards the player with caps and a reputation boost.
