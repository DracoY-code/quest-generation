<|begin_quest|>
<|begin_objective|>
Improve the efficiency of the Gecko nuclear power plant to provide clean energy to Vault City.
<|end_objective|>
<|begin_tasks|>
Obtain the optimization data from Vault City’s computer.
Upload the optimization data to the reactor’s mainframe.
<|end_tasks|>
<|begin_quest_giver|>
Festus: A ghoul technician in Gecko who wants the power plant to run at full efficiency.
<|end_quest_giver|>
<|begin_task_locations|>
Vault City: A technologically advanced settlement with strict citizenship rules.
Gecko: A ghoul-run settlement with a nuclear power plant.
<|end_task_locations|>
<|begin_rewards|>
Caps: A monetary reward for successfully optimizing the reactor. (amount: 1000)
Improved relations with Vault City: Vault City will be more willing to trade with Gecko. (amount: NONE)
<|end_rewards|>
<|end_quest|>
<|begin_description|>
The nuclear power plant in Gecko is running inefficiently. By obtaining and installing optimization data from Vault City, you can improve its performance and reduce tensions between the two settlements.
<|end_description|>
