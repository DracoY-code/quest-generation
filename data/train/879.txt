<|end_objective|>
<|begin_tasks|>

<|end_tasks|>

<|end_task_locations|>
<|begin_rewards|>

Tandi, the daughter of Aradesh, has been kidnapped by the Khans. The Vault Dweller must infiltrate the raider camp and rescue her, either through diplomacy, stealth, or combat. The outcome will determine the fate of Shady Sands and the player's standing in the wasteland.
