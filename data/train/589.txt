<|begin_quest|>
<|begin_objective|>
Repair the broken water pump in Necropolis to restore the ghouls' water supply.
<|end_objective|>
<|begin_tasks|>
Locate the broken water pump in the Watershed.
Find the necessary parts to repair the pump.
Repair the pump to restore water flow.
<|end_tasks|>
<|begin_quest_giver|>
Set: Leader of the Necropolis ghouls, seeking a solution for the water crisis.
<|end_quest_giver|>
<|begin_task_locations|>
Watershed, Necropolis: An underground facility containing the water pump, currently in disrepair.
<|end_task_locations|>
<|begin_rewards|>
Caps: Payment for successfully fixing the water pump. (amount: 500)
Experience Points: Earned for repairing the pump and helping the ghouls. (amount: 1000)
Ghoul Favor: Increased standing with the ghouls of Necropolis. (amount: 1)
<|end_rewards|>
<|end_quest|>
The water pump in Necropolis has broken down, cutting off the ghouls' water supply. The player must find a way to repair it to ensure the survival of the Necropolis inhabitants.
